# The Outline of the Exit Interview for the Evaluation Study

**Part 1: General privacy management perceptions**

1. Could you describe your process for managing personal information when using these different technologies? What actions, if any, did you take to control the information stored in the LLM's memory?
2. When deciding what information to retain or remove, what factors influenced your decisions? Can you walk me through your thought process with an example?

**Part 2: Detailed feedback on MemoAnalyzer**

1. As you use MemoAnalyzer, what would be your typical interaction flow?
2. Please describe your experience with the notification features.
3. (If observed) We observed that you interacted with the expandable widgets in MemoAnalyzer. What were your expectations when you clicked to expand them, and what was your experience with the information presented?
4. Can you describe how you approached modifying the text that was identified as containing private information? What kinds of changes did you make and why?
5. How, if at all, did using MemoAnalyzer influence your understanding of the potential privacy implications of the stored memory?
6. Based on your experience, what do you see as the primary strengths and weaknesses of the MemoAnalyzer prototype?

**Part 3: Comparative experience on alternative techniques**

1. Could you discuss the respective advantages and disadvantages of the other three techniques in the context of managing your privacy?
2. Were there any specific features in the other technologies that you found particularly helpful or unhelpful for managing your privacy? Please describe them.
