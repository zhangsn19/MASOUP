# The Outline of the Exit Interview for the Evaluation Study

We outlined the exit interview questions below. When asking the participants, we framed the questions as non-leading, unbiased, and open-ended.

**Part 1: General privacy management perceptions**

1. Could you describe your process for managing personal information when using these different settings? What actions, if any, did you take to control the information stored in the LLM's memory?
2. When deciding what information to retain or remove, what factors influenced your decisions? Can you walk me through your thought process with an example?
3. In what ways, if any, did the different settings change how much personal information you were willing to share over the course of the study?

**Part 2: Feedback on the Proactive setting**

1. As you use the proactive setting, what would be your typical interaction flow?
2. Please describe your experience with the notification features.
3. (If applicable) As you said, you interacted with the expandable widgets in the proactive setting. What were your expectations when you clicked to expand them, and what was your experience with the information presented?
4. How did you approach editing text that the system flagged as private? What was your reasoning for the specific changes you made?
5. How, if at all, did using the proactive setting influence your understanding of the potential privacy implications of the stored memory?

**Part 3: Feedback on the Automatic setting**

1. When, if at all, did you engage with the notifications or the memory management panel?
2. (If applicable) When using the memory management panel, how would you decide on the functions to use, and why?

**Part 4: Feedback on the Cluster setting**

1. When, if at all, would you interact with the cluster function, and how would you determine the cluster granularity?
2. How would you approach the use of other functions, like edit, delete, reloading, and grouping?

**Part 5: Feedback on the Drag setting**

1. When and how would you use the drag, summarize, edit, visible functions, and why?

**Part 6: Recommendations**

1. Do you have any other comments or suggestions for these settings, or for memory management in general?
