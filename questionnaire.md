# The subjective rating entries for the evaluation study

We detailed the subjective rating entries for the evaluation study. Notably, the abbreviated name of the evaluation entry was only included in the paper rather than shown to participants. Besides, in most of the time we let participants rate the system as a whole rather than only rate the privacy protection modules, as the privacy protection effectiveness may affect the task utility, and thereby affect the satisfaction and helpfulness.

Learing efficinecy: Is the system easy to learn? (1: Very difficult to learn -- 7: Very easy to learn)

Overall satisfaction: How satisfied are you with the system as a whole? (1: Very dissatisfied -- 7: Very satisfied)

Please rate whether you agree with the following sayings.
Perceived control: I feel I can effectively control this system.
(1: Strongly disagree -- 7: Strongly agree)

Please rate whether you agree with the following sayings.
Privacy risk awareness: I believe this system makes me aware of privacy risks. (1: Strongly disagree -- 7: Strongly agree)

Please rate whether you agree with the following sayings.
Perceived privacy protection: I believe this system helps me resolve privacy issues. (1: Strongly disagree -- 7: Strongly agree)
